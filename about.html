From trusted roots to a more complete approach

Ball Financial is built on the foundation of Ball Insurance Agency — a long-standing presence in Sandusky, Michigan. That history is rooted in relationships, trust, and helping people protect what matters.

Over time, it became clear that many clients needed more than insurance. They needed guidance across investments, retirement planning, taxes, and long-term financial decisions.

Ball Financial was created as a natural evolution — a way to provide a more complete, coordinated financial planning experience while maintaining the same level of trust the agency was built on.
